Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is CS Ed Week?

A: CS Ed Week is held in recognition of the birthday of computing pioneer Grace Hopper from December 7-13. During this week, students have the opportunity to learn more about the power of computer science through educational challenges and interactive demo sessions.


Q: Where is the event happening?

A: The event takes place in Davis Hall, University at Buffalo North Campus.


Q: How can I sign up?

A: If you are a current UB student, select UB Students from the menu and click on the sign up button; otherwise, select Grade 1-12 Students in order to sign up.


Q: I still have more questions, who do I contact?

A: If you have any questions, please send us an email at cse-edweek@buffalo.edu.
